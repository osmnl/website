Support us

OpenStreetMap mainly depends on donations for financing. For PayPal donations there is a seperate website: donate.openstreetmap.org. Our thanks and eternal glory is your part. If you prefer to make a donation in another way, see the Donations page on the wiki. Another way to make a financial donation is to become a member of the OpenStreetMap Foundation. This costs 15 pounds per year and provides, besides discounts for the annual State of the Map congres, control within this organisation. In the above situations you support the worldwide OpenStreetMap community. The Dutch community operates, financially speaking, entirely independently of the international community.
